Tips & Links

🚗 Car rental: Arnold Clark — one-way Edinburgh (EDI) → Inverness (INV). Book early for June.
📷 Golden hour: June sunsets ~10pm, sunrises ~4:30am — pack a headtorch for early starts.
🌦 Weather: Always carry full waterproofs. June averages 12–16°C. Wind is the real enemy.
🛣 NC500 accommodation: Book at least 3–4 months ahead — fills up fast in June.
🚧 Single-track roads: Most Highland roads are single-track with passing places. Add 30–50% to Google Maps estimates.
🦟 Midges: Peak midge season — bring repellent (Smidge brand is best). Worst at dawn/dusk near water.
⛽ Petrol: Fill up whenever you see a station in the far north — gaps of 50+ km are common.
